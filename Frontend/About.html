About the University of Liberia

Committed to Academic Excellence and National Development

Our Mission and Vision

The University of Liberia, established in 1862, is the premier institution of higher learning in Liberia. Our mission is to provide quality education, foster research, and contribute to the socio-economic development of Liberia through innovative programs and community engagement. We envision a future where our graduates are leaders and innovators, driving positive change across the nation and beyond.

With a rich history of academic rigor and public service, the University of Liberia continues to be a beacon of knowledge, empowering generations of students to achieve their full potential and serve their communities.

Student Data Records on This Site

This website features a directory of university student profiles, designed to provide a clear and accessible overview of our student body. The data presented for each student is carefully curated to offer essential information while respecting privacy.

Each student profile card includes the following details:

  • Student ID: A unique identifier assigned to each enrolled student, ensuring accurate record-keeping.
  • Name: The full name of the student.
  • Department: The academic department or faculty to which the student belongs (e.g., Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Business Administration).
  • Level: Indicates the student's academic standing, such as Undergraduate (with year) or Graduate (with degree program like MBA, PhD).
  • Contact: A professional email address for communication, facilitating connections for academic or professional purposes.

This directory aims to showcase the diversity and academic pursuits of our students, fostering a sense of community and providing relevant information in a professional format.
